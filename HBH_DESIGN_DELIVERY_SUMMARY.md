## 💡 Key Insights & Recommendations

### 1. **Hybrid Design Approach Works Best**
The recommended approach combines:
- **Dashboard first** — Clear overview and status at a glance
- **Room-based navigation** — Intuitive spatial organization
- **Checklist tracking** — Granular progress visibility

This allows users to see big picture while also managing details.

### 2. **Decision Paralysis is the Core Problem**
The app needs to:
- Show next steps (not all options)
- Provide smart defaults and recommendations
- Make comparisons easy (side-by-side)
- Celebrate progress (completed decisions)

### 3. **Collaboration is Critical**
Success depends on:
- Easy sharing with architects, contractors, family
- Clear visibility into who decided what
- Threaded discussions on decisions
- Version history and change tracking

### 4. **Timeline Transparency Builds Confidence**
Showing:
- Phase dependencies and critical path
- Impact of each decision on overall timeline
- Next steps and what's blocking progress
- Realistic timelines (accounts for lead times)

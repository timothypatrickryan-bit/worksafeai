## Source Selection Rationale

**Coverage:** 
- **Construction & Capacity:** DCK, Datacenter Dynamics, Light Reading (official announcements + deep intel)
- **Fiber & Subsea:** TeleGeography (market leader), Light Reading, Fierce Telecom
- **Strategic News:** Reuters, Bloomberg (tier-1 official announcements)
- **Primary Sources:** AWS/Google/Meta/Azure blogs (verify all major announcements)

**Verification Hierarchy:**
1. Official company blogs/press releases (100% trusted)
2. Industry filings (SEC, utilities, cable consortiums)
3. Tier-1 beat reporters with named sources
4. Industry analyst quotes (with attribution)
5. Unattributed rumors (flag as such)

**Freshness:**
- Tier 1 sources checked 3-5x daily (morning scan: 6-8 AM EST)
- Tier 2 sources checked 1x daily
- Tier 3 (official) checked as part of daily routine
- Tier 4 (digests) checked 1x weekly

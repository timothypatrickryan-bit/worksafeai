## IMPACT SCORING SYSTEM

Use this 5-point scale to prioritize stories:

**5 = CRITICAL** 
- Major capex announcement (>$5B)
- New region/market entry (hyperscaler's first in geography)
- Subsea cable consortium forms or major cable approved
- Strategic partnership affecting infrastructure at scale
- Supply chain/material shortage affecting all hyperscalers

**4 = HIGH**
- Significant facility expansion (100+ MWs power)
- New subsea cable route announced
- Major partner announced (utilities, carriers, equipment vendor)
- Sustainability requirement materially impacts timeline
- Consolidation/acquisition with infrastructure implications

**3 = MEDIUM**
- Facility construction/expansion update (routine progress)
- Regional facility announced (not first in region)
- Vendor partnership (infrastructure-specific equipment)
- Fiber route expansion (terrestrial)
- Power/cooling tech advancement

**2 = LOW**
- Facility minor update
- Staffing announcement (infrastructure division)
- Small partner announcement
- Incremental capacity upgrade

**1 = MONITORING**
- Rumor/unconfirmed report
- Early-stage planning/RFP
- Regulatory filing (no announcement yet)
- Competitor move (not direct action)

## Project Overview

**WorkSafeAI: Intelligence That Protects**

- **What:** AI-powered workplace safety and hazard intelligence platform
- **Who:** Construction & utility contractors, safety teams, project managers
- **Why:** Reduce workplace injuries and improve safety compliance with AI
- **How:** Job Task Safety Analysis (JTSA) with Claude AI hazard identification

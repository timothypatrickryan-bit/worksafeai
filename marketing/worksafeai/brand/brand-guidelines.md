# WorkSafeAI Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Name & Tagline
- **Name:** WorkSafeAI
- **Tagline:** Intelligence That Protects
- **Subtitle:** AI-powered workplace safety & hazard intelligence

### Brand Promise
WorkSafeAI empowers safety teams to identify and mitigate workplace hazards before they become incidents, using advanced AI to protect workers and reduce risk.

## Tone & Voice

### Brand Voice Characteristics
- **Professional but Approachable** → Expert, not condescending
- **Clear & Direct** → No jargon without explanation
- **Confident & Optimistic** → "We can solve this" attitude
- **Safety-Focused** → Always centered on protecting people

### Example Messaging

| Context | Good | Avoid |
|---------|------|-------|
| Feature description | "Identify hazards before workers get hurt" | "Hazard detection system" |
| Error message | "We couldn't analyze that task. Try rephrasing it." | "Error 500: Invalid input" |
| Success message | "✓ 3 hazards identified. Review and mitigate." | "Processing complete" |
| Call-to-action | "Protect Your Team" | "Submit" |

## Do's & Don'ts

### Do's ✅
- ✅ Use safety metaphors (shield, protection, defense)
- ✅ Emphasize people-first outcomes (worker safety first)
- ✅ Use active voice (AI identifies hazards)
- ✅ Include statistics/proof points when possible
- ✅ Show real-world scenarios and use cases

### Don'ts ❌
- ❌ Make it sound overly technical
- ❌ Use fear-mongering or excessive warnings
- ❌ Ignore the human element (compliance isn't the goal, safety is)
- ❌ Use outdated imagery (hard hats alone don't sell)
- ❌ Make unrealistic claims about AI replacing human judgment

## Marketing Applications

### Website Copy
- Hero: "Intelligence That Protects"
- Subheader: "Identify workplace hazards with AI before they cause injury"
- CTA: "Start Your Free Trial" or "Schedule a Demo"

### Social Media
- Tone: Professional + Educational + Motivational
- Content Mix: 60% Education, 25% Product, 15% Company/Culture
- Hashtags: #WorkplaceSafety #AI #SafetyFirst #Construction #HazardIdentification

### Email Marketing
- Subject lines: Action-oriented, benefit-focused
- Example: "How [Company Name] Reduced Safety Incidents by 40%"

### Sales Collateral
- Focus on ROI: Risk reduction, compliance, cost savings
- Audience: Safety managers, project managers, owners
- Key talking points: Speed, accuracy, ease of use, cost-effectiveness

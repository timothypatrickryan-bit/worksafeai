# WorkSafeAI Content Strategy

**Goal:** Position WorkSafeAI as the go-to expert in AI-powered workplace safety, while driving traffic, leads, and conversions.

### Blog Post Structure

**Format A: How-To Guide** (500-800 words)
```
1. Compelling headline
2. Intro (hook + what they'll learn)
3. Why this matters (1 paragraph)
4. Step-by-step instructions (5-7 steps)
5. Common mistakes section
6. Conclusion
7. CTA (Free trial or demo)
```

**Example:**
"How to Conduct a JTSA in Under 10 Minutes
- Hook: "Most teams spend 30+ minutes analyzing a single task. You don't have to."
- Intro: Learn the 5-step system that cuts analysis time in half
- Step 1: Gather your team
- Step 2: Describe the task in detail
- Step 3: Identify hazards
- Step 4: Determine controls
- Step 5: Document and review
- CTA: "Let AI handle the analysis - try WorkSafeAI free"
```

**Format B: Statistics/Data Post** (800-1200 words)
```
1. Compelling headline with stat
2. Intro (why this matters)
3. Key findings (5-8 major stats)
4. Deep dive per stat (paragraph + context)
5. What this means for you (actionable takeaways)
6. What to do about it (next steps)
7. CTA (Subscribe for updates)
```

**Example:**
"2026 Construction Safety Statistics Every Manager Must Know
- Opening stat: "40% of construction injuries are preventable with proper analysis"
- Why it matters: Cost + people impact
- 5 key statistics from BLS, OSHA, industry reports
- What this means for safety teams
- What you should do: Audit your process, consider AI tools
```

**Format C: Case Study** (1000-1500 words)
```
1. Compelling headline
2. The challenge (what problem the company faced)
3. The solution (how they solved it)
4. The results (metrics, improvement)
5. Key learnings (what others can take away)
6. Conclusion
7. CTA (Schedule demo)
```

**Example:**
"How ABC Construction Reduced Safety Incidents by 35% in 6 Months
- Challenge: Manual JTSAs taking 30 min each, missing hazards, high incident rate
- Solution: Implemented WorkSafeAI + trained team on AI-powered analysis
- Results: 85% time reduction, 35% fewer incidents, better compliance
- What they learned: AI + human judgment is the winning combo
```

**Format D: Opinion/Thought Leadership** (800-1200 words)
```
1. Bold, controversial headline
2. The problem statement
3. Why most solutions fail (critique)
4. Your perspective (the contrarian view)
5. How to think about it differently
6. Practical implications
7. Conclusion
8. CTA (Join the conversation)
```

**Example:**
"Your Safety Program is Broken (And Here's Why)
- Problem: Injury rates climbing despite more regulation
- Why traditional approaches fail: Too slow, too manual, miss hazards
- The contrarian view: Safety needs AI, not more bureaucracy
- How to fix it: Shift from reactive to proactive with AI analysis
```

## Content Analytics & Optimization

### What to Track

**Blog Metrics:**
- Page views & unique visitors
- Average time on page
- Bounce rate (target: <50%)
- Click-through rate to CTA (target: 5%+)
- Conversions (signups from post)
- Organic search traffic
- Search keywords bringing traffic

**Social Metrics:**

| Metric | LinkedIn | Twitter | Instagram |
|--------|----------|---------|-----------|
| Impressions | Track | Track | Track |
| Engagement Rate | >3% | >2% | >5% |
| Clicks (CTR) | >1% | >2% | >3% |
| Follower Growth | 10-15/week | 5-10/week | 5-8/week |
| Mentions | Monitor | Track replies | Track DMs |
| Conversions | Track link clicks | Track clicks | Track link clicks |

### Optimization Strategy

**Monthly Content Review:**
- [ ] Identify top 3 performing posts per platform
- [ ] Analyze: Topic? Format? CTA? Timing?
- [ ] Identify top 3 underperforming posts
- [ ] Analyze: Why did they flop? Adjust.
- [ ] A/B test: Headlines, CTAs, posting times
- [ ] Update underperforming content or repurpose

**Quarterly Strategy Review:**
- [ ] Trending topics in industry - adjust pillars
- [ ] Customer feedback - what they want to learn
- [ ] Competitor content - what's working for them
- [ ] KPI performance - are we hitting targets?
- [ ] Resource capacity - do we need to adjust cadence?

## Repurposing Strategy

**One blog post = 5+ pieces of content**

Example: Blog post "How to Conduct a JTSA in 5 Steps"

**Repurposing:**
1. **LinkedIn Carousel** - 5 slides, one per step
2. **Twitter Thread** - Summarize 5 steps in tweet thread
3. **Infographic** - Visual representation of 5 steps
4. **Short Video** - 60-second walkthrough
5. **LinkedIn Article** - Link + summary
6. **Email** - Send to subscriber list
7. **Webinar Slide** - Use as presentation
8. **PDF Checklist** - Free download lead magnet

**Multiplier Effect:** 1 piece of original content = 7+ pieces of distribution

# WorkSafeAI Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## Overview

This document defines the critical metrics we track to measure WorkSafeAI's success across customer acquisition, product engagement, retention, and business health.

---

## Business Metrics

### Revenue & Growth

| KPI | Q1 Target | Q2 Target | Q3 Target | Q4 Target | Owner |
|-----|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| **MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue) | $2K | $5K | $15K | $30K | CEO |
| **ARR** (Annual Recurring Revenue) | $24K | $60K | $180K | $360K | CFO |
| **Growth Rate** (MoM) | - | 150% | 200% | 100% | CEO |
| **Total Customers** | 30 | 75 | 200 | 400 | CEO |

### Unit Economics

| KPI | Target | Notes |
|-----|--------|-------|
| **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost) | <$500 | (Total marketing spend / new customers) |
| **LTV** (Lifetime Value) | $1000+ | (Average revenue per customer × 36 months) |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | 3:1 or better | Healthy: 3+:1 |
| **CAC Payback Period** | <6 months | How long to recoup acquisition cost |
| **Churn Rate** | <5% monthly | Monthly customer loss |
| **Expansion Revenue** | 15-20% | Revenue from existing customers (upsells) |

### Profitability

| KPI | Target | Notes |
|-----|--------|-------|
| **Gross Margin** | >80% | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue |
| **Operating Margin** | -50% (Year 1) | Expected to improve as scale grows |
| **Break-even Date** | Q3 2027 | Target profitability timeline |

## Tracking & Reporting

### Dashboard Cadence
- **Daily:** Website metrics, support tickets, critical issues
- **Weekly:** Sales pipeline, active users, churn alerts
- **Monthly:** Full KPI review, unit economics, customer health
- **Quarterly:** Board review, strategic adjustments

### Tools
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, Mixpanel
- **CRM:** Pipedrive, HubSpot
- **Financial:** Stripe, custom dashboards
- **Customer:** Surveys, feedback tools

### Review Process
- **Weekly standup:** Sales + Marketing + Product
- **Monthly review:** Leadership + Department heads
- **Quarterly planning:** Strategic adjustments based on KPI trends

## Red Flags & Triggers for Action

| Metric | Red Flag | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Churn Rate** | >5% monthly | Investigate customer satisfaction, improve retention |
| **Trial Conversion** | <10% | Improve onboarding, adjust pricing, messaging |
| **MRR Growth** | <10% MoM | Increase marketing spend, improve sales process |
| **CAC** | >$750 | Reduce marketing spend, refocus channels |
| **NPS** | <40 | Product improvements, customer success focus |
| **Response Time** | >8 hours | Hire support staff, improve systems |

---

## Success Definition (Year 1)

✅ **Achieved if:**
- MRR reaches $30K (from $0)
- 400+ customers acquired
- >50% trial conversion rate
- NPS score 50+
- <5% monthly churn
- Positive unit economics (LTV:CAC 3:1+)
- Brand established in industry

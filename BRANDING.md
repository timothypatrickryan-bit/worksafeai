# WorkSafeAI Branding Guide

## Official Name & Tagline
- **Product Name:** WorkSafeAI
- **Tagline:** Intelligence That Protects
- **Full Tagline:** AI-powered workplace safety & hazard intelligence

## Brand Identity

### Core Values
- 🛡️ **Protection** — Keeping workplaces safe
- 🧠 **Intelligence** — AI-powered insights
- 🚀 **Innovation** — Modern safety solutions
- 📊 **Transparency** — Clear risk visibility

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Blue:** `#1e40af` (trust, professionalism)
- **Accent Cyan:** `#06b6d4` (modern, intelligent)
- **Success Green:** `#10b981` (safety, completion)
- **Warning Amber:** `#f59e0b` (caution, alerts)
- **Danger Red:** `#dc2626` (critical risks)

### Typography
- **Fonts:** System font stack (-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Segoe UI, etc.)
- **Weights:** Bold for headings, semibold for emphasis, regular for body

## Usage Guidelines

### App Naming
- Full name in marketing: **WorkSafeAI: Intelligence That Protects**
- Short name in UI: **WorkSafeAI**
- Package name: `worksafeai-frontend`
- Domain (future): `worksafeai.com`

### Messaging
- **Dashboard subtitle:** "Your workplace safety intelligence dashboard"
- **Login subtitle:** "AI-powered workplace safety & hazard intelligence"
- **Register subtitle:** "Protect your workplace with AI intelligence"

### Feature Naming
- "Analyze Workplace Hazards" (instead of "Create New JTSA")
- "Safety Projects" (instead of "View Projects")
- "Safety Assessments" (instead of "JTSAs")

## How to Use This List

**Daily Briefing Workflow:**
- **Primary Check (30 min):** Data Center Knowledge, Light Reading, CNBC (Tier 1)
- **Secondary Scan (15 min):** ZDNET, key industry feeds as needed
- **Deep Dives (optional):** IDC/Omdia/Gartner research for trend validation
- **Cross-Check:** TeleGeography, The Register for verification of major stories

**Source Rotation:**
- Refresh this list quarterly (April, July, October, January)
- Track which sources break stories first → weight accordingly
- Note: TheInformation is paywalled; access via subscription or alert briefings
- Omdia/IDC reports often summarized in press releases; monitor for free versions

- **Why it matters:** Flips the traditional site-selection playbook. Texas/Southwest emerging as "AI Valley" due to grid capacity and renewable potential. Companies solving energy problems become winners.

### **3. Interconnect Technology Partnership (Score: 4.2/5.0) — SECONDARY**
- **Source:** LightSpeed Photonics + Infraeo MoU; Data Center Knowledge
- **Headline:** New near-packaged optics (NPO) technology solving AI data-movement bottlenecks. Partnership includes US pilots + India supply chain.
- **Why it matters:** AI bottleneck shifting from compute to data movement within racks. NPO provides practical middle ground between copper limits and complex co-packaged optics.
- **Competitive implication:** Hyperscalers that integrate NPO technology first gain efficiency and latency advantages.
- **Trend signal:** Infrastructure innovation is now driven by interconnect/data-movement challenges, not chip speed.

### **4. ARM Processors Expanding in Europe (Score: 4.0/5.0) — SECONDARY**
- **Source:** Ampere Computing expansion; Data Center Knowledge
- **Headline:** Ampere ARM processors launching across Oracle + regional European cloud providers (Scaleway, Hetzner, etc.)
- **Why it matters:** Power efficiency (tokens-per-watt) becoming primary purchasing driver, not raw performance. ARM adoption following hyperscaler playbook proven by AWS.
- **Northeast angle:** If ARM adoption spreads to North America, could disrupt traditional x86 supply chains. Watch for similar announcements from US regional cloud providers.
- **Trend signal:** Hyperscaler infrastructure models are spreading to regional providers—consolidation around power-efficient architectures accelerating.

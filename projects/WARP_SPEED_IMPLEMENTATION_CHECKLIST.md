## RISK MANAGEMENT & CONTINGENCIES

### RISK #1: VP Sales Recruitment Delays

**If VP Sales not hired by April 30, 2026:**

1. **Interim Option (0-90 days):**
   - Hire fractional VP Sales (consultant) for 90 days
   - Cost: $30-40K for 3 months
   - Allows time to find permanent hire
   - Keeps sales function moving

2. **Internal Option:**
   - Promote strong AE to interim VP Sales
   - Hire external VP within 90 days
   - Risk: May not be ready for leadership role

3. **Accelerate AE Hiring:**
   - If VP Sales delayed, hire 2-3 strong AEs immediately
   - Operate without VP for 3-4 months
   - Risk: No sales leadership, coordination gaps

**GO/NO-GO Decision:** By May 15, 2026
- If VP Sales hired or consultant in place: GO
- If no plan in place: NO-GO on Wave 1 (reassess)

---

### RISK #2: Sales Pipeline Slower Than Expected

**If pipeline <$1M by June 30, 2026:**

1. **Accelerate Prospecting:**
   - Hire SDR immediately (outbound lead gen)
   - VP Sales conduct prospecting directly
   - Partner with industry analysts for introductions

2. **Adjust Strategy:**
   - Shift to regional/colocation focus (faster cycles)
   - Increase marketing investment
   - Attend more industry events

3. **Cost Control:**
   - Delay non-essential Wave 2 hires (e.g., delay Procurement Manager)
   - Slow field team expansion (Wave 3)
   - Adjust revenue targets downward for Year 1

**GO/NO-GO Decision:** By August 31, 2026
- If pipeline catch-up by August: GO with Wave 2
- If still weak: NO-GO on full Wave 2 (reduce scope)

---

### RISK #3: Operations Execution Issues

**If project delivery problems (margin, schedule, quality):**

1. **Immediate Actions:**
   - VP Operations conduct project health review (root cause)
   - Increase project management oversight
   - Slow new project commitments (ensure quality first)

2. **Capability Gaps:**
   - Accelerate engineer/PM hiring if capacity issue
   - Bring in project management consultant (coaching)
   - Improve estimating/bidding process

3. **Scope Reduction:**
   - Reduce bid on larger projects initially
   - Focus on smaller, well-defined projects first
   - Build reference customers with "quick wins"

**GO/NO-GO Decision:** By October 31, 2026
- If issues resolved: GO with Wave 3
- If ongoing issues: NO-GO on Wave 3 (fix operations first)

---

### RISK #4: Key Person Departure

**If VP Sales or VP Operations departs (e.g., June-September 2026):**

1. **Immediate Actions:**
   - Have #2 person trained (can take over)
   - If unprepared, hire interim consultant (60-90 days)
   - Communicate transparently with team (no panic)

2. **Recruitment:**
   - Engage search firm immediately (don't rely on new hire to recruit their own replacement)
   - Consider internal promotion + external advisor

3. **Business Continuity:**
   - Ops: Likely can continue (project PMs take over)
   - Sales: More critical (need immediate leadership)

**Prevention:**
- Competitive package (salary + bonus + equity)
- Growth opportunities
- Strong culture & relationships
- Regular feedback & coaching

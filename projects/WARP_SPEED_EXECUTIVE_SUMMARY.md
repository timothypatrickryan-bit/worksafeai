### CRITICAL RISKS

- Flexible cost structure (variable labor vs. fixed overhead)
- Multiple customer segments (don't depend on hyperscalers alone)
- Strong leadership team + clear accountability

## QUESTIONS & CLARIFICATIONS

**Q: Isn't 30 people in 12 months too aggressive?**
A: No. This is normal for a growth company with $650K+ investment and strong market opportunity. Pro-Tel is targeting 5-7x revenue growth; team size scales proportionally.

**Q: What if we can't hire fast enough?**
A: Adjust Wave timelines; prioritize sales first (other functions can wait). Hire SVPs to build sub-teams. Use interim consultants if needed. Monthly recalibration allows for adjustment.

**Q: What if hyperscaler deals don't happen?**
A: Diversify into colocation/regional deals (shorter cycles, lower risk). These segments are 30-40% of market TAM; can still build to $60M+ revenue.

**Q: What if the market slows?**
A: Monthly reviews allow for quick pivot. Can reduce Wave 3 field hiring if pipeline weakens. Build long-term contracts (not spot deals).

**Q: How do we maintain culture with 3x team growth?**
A: Invest in onboarding, define culture early, hire strong HR person (Wave 2), weekly leadership sync, all-hands transparency, strong manager training.

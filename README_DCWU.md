## 🚨 WHAT IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG?

**Source goes offline?** → Switch to RSS feed or alternative source (backup plan in framework)

**Tim doesn't approve email by 10:30 AM Friday?** → Scout & Steven stand by to adjust; hold dispatch until approved

**Breaking news Friday morning?** → Scout alerts Steven; add to "Breaking This Week" section if major Northeast story

**Citation conflict (sources disagree)?** → Flag in email ("Timing unclear; sources differ"). Scout contacts both press offices for clarification by Monday.

**Source accuracy drops?** → Log in Source Tracker; consider moving to Tier 2-3 or removing

**Pro-Tel relevance is missing?** → Steven edits to connect story to expansion strategy. If can't connect, consider removing story.

## Guidelines

✅ **Do:**
- Keep it short and punchy
- Link trends to real business impact
- Ask genuine questions
- Share learnings and perspectives
- Vary between optimistic and cautious takes

❌ **Don't:**
- Over-promote (no constant sales pitches)
- Get too technical without context
- Spread unverified rumors
- Make sweeping predictions
- Ignore sourcing (cite reports/announcements)

🚀 HOW IT WORKS:

Every Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday @ 9:00 AM EST:

1. macOS launchd wakes up automatically
2. Calls linkedin-auto-post-brave.sh
3. Script queries Brave Search for trending articles
4. Generates professional LinkedIn post with sources
5. Saves to .linkedin-current-post.json
6. Logs activity to .linkedin-launchd.log
7. (Optional) Browser relay auto-posts to LinkedIn
